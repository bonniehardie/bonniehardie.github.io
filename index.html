Originally from Nashville, Tennessee, I graduated from Guilford College with a Bachelor of Music in Piano Performance and a minor in Environmental Studies. I then attended Emory University where I earned a Master of Music. I taught students of all ages and abilities in Classical Piano, Voice, Music Theory, Music History, Aural Skills, Composition, Sight-Singing, and Conducting, both one-on-one and in a classroom setting.

While I loved teaching, I longed to spend more of my time creating. It was then that I found the captivating world of programming. The more I learned about development, the more hooked I was, so I enrolled in App Academy, a rigorous bootcamp, to sharpen my skills.
